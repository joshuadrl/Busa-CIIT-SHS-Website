SPECIALIZED LEARNING

Both general academic and specialist courses are adjusted to be strand-specific to the fullest extent. Lessons are designed to offer you a better understanding of how your field of study links to the broader academic subjects you are studying. You will be taught subject material that is specific and highly useful in applying theoretical understanding to practical actions.


groups

COLLABORATION BETWEEN SUBJECTS

The CIDAM (Classroom Instruction Delivery Alignment Map) ensures that all subjects collaborate to optimize their activities. This is developed to reduce your output needs and provide you with more opportunities to present high-quality work.


assessment

PERFORMANCE BASED ASSESSMENT

Our curriculum is skills-based. When assigning tasks, all topics adhere to the Performance-Based Assessment Tasks (PBAT) policy. Instead of taking theoretical examinations, you'll be required to submit projects that put your teachings into practice, and you'll be graded on how well you do.


terminal

DIVERSE PROGRAMMING LANGUAGE EDUCATION

The Department of Education (DepEd) has mandated that Java and.Net be the centerpiece of the programming language curriculum. We do, however, encourage that you learn and use various programming languages, such as Python and C.
